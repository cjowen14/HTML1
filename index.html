Why The Last Jedi Isn't A "Dumpster Fire"

The Last Jedi is the most divisive Star Wars movie and one of the most divisive movies of all time. I don't think it deserves the hate that it gets. It seems that because there are a few things people didn't like about the movie, they nitpick and make everything seem like it was bad but that just isn't the case. Is the movie perfect? Absolutely not. But none of the Star Wars movies are perfect by any means. While TLJ has a few issues, its pros far outweigh those issues and create a fantastic cinematic experience.
